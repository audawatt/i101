Process

Project Reasearch
Since we used Andrew’s pick for innovator, we already has some research done. To expand upon it, we broke down different things we wanted to highlight about Annie Easley’s life. Different members researched different topics such as biography, contributions, and future promise. We then all collectively added it to a Word doc and cross-examined our work. Finally, we looked over the project as a whole and added any final details. The strategy of breaking research down and then zooming out again helped make our work efficient, and we got everything done in one meeting.
Website Wireframe
We continued to use the process of splitting the work among us. Each of us worked on an individual frame, with the innovator page being tackled by two people since it was the most complicated. As we finished up, we began to work between frames as well, adding out own insight to the entire project overall.
